Robert Half Technology is a Silver sponsor for our Robocode V event. Robert Half Technology is an IT staffing firm in Indianapolis, specializing in the placement of technology professionals.
Anchor Point Technology Resources is a sponsor for our Robocode V event. At Anchor Point we offer top quality IT and Engineering solutions. Whether you are looking for short or long term answers to your IT and engineering needs we are committed to providing the most qualified resources and services.
Lowder Graphic Design Inc. created our logo and other graphics used on the site. They design and create beautiful and functional business cards, letterhead, brochures, newsletters, logos and more to promote your company
Knowledge Services was our facility (and beverage) sponsor for many years. They are a professional services organization providing training and education strategies, meeting and training facilities and contract staffing services in order to make people more productive and advance your business.
