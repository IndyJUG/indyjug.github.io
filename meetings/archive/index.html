Welcome to the Indianapolis Java User Group web site.

Who Are We?

IndyJUG is a group of professionals and hobbyists in area of Indianapolis, Indiana, USA who share an interest in Java technology. Many of us work with Java every day at work. Others are students or professionals who have not yet worked with Java.

What do we do?

We meet periodically to share ideas about Java and tools for Java. We have a small library of books on Java and related technology. You can check out the books at the meetings.

Getting Involved

To get involved: (1) come to our next meeting, (2) join in the discussion in our yahoo Discussion Forum for discussion and announcements. By joining the forum, you'll be sure to receive announcements and related information about IndyJUG.
