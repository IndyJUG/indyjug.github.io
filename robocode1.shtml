We are pleased to announce that the first annual IndyJUG Robocode Competition will be held at our November 17th meeting.

Fusion Alliance is sponsoring the first occurrence of this competition which we hope will become a fun and anticipated event for the IndyJUG.

Besides the obvious bragging rights and resume decorations that the winners will secure, there will also be $500 in cash prizes for the winners.

The prize money will be as follows:
1st place: $300
2nd place: $125
3rd place: $75

The rules for the competition are simple:
- Entrants must be a member of the IndyJUG yahoo group. (So sign up already!)
- One bot per entrant.
- Write your own bot.

Bots must be submitted by Midnight of 11/15/10 (email them to indyjug@gmail.com).
The competition will be conducted at the November IndyJUG meeting on a machine provided by the IndyJUG.
The winners will based on the total number of points they accumulate using the normal Robocode scoring system.

More information (as well as the code) can be found at http://robocode.sourceforge.net.

Please feel free to post questions about this to the list.

Write some code and win some cash for the holidays!
